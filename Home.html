About Us

Established in 1998, MYW Technologies has grown into one of Myanmar’s leading import and export enterprises, with over two decades of experience driving trade, technology, and industrial development. From the outset, the company has been committed to fostering growth across multiple sectors, building long-term partnerships, and creating value for both local and international stakeholders.

Through continuous innovation, strategic planning, and resilience, MYW Technologies has achieved significant milestones across industries, with an annual trading volume now reaching USD 30 million. This performance positions the company as a key contributor to Myanmar’s economic growth and its trade relationships with the global market.

Entering the 21st century, MYW Technologies embraced new opportunities by modernizing business strategies, adopting advanced operational systems, and cultivating professional expertise. The company has expanded its focus on project contracting, international trade, and local sales, developing strong capabilities across sectors including: Machinery & Industrial Equipment, Pharmaceuticals & Healthcare, Energy & Mining, IT & Electronics, Construction & Infrastructure Development.

Leveraging this multi-sector expertise, MYW Technologies has built robust service and information networks connecting Myanmar to major global markets, ensuring clients and partners benefit from international best practices and reliable trade channels.

With more than 20 years of consistent growth, MYW Technologies is recognized for its credibility, financial strength, and professional excellence. The company remains dedicated to creating opportunities, driving innovation, and supporting Myanmar’s integration into the global economy.

Bruker Introduces Next-Generation 80 MHz Benchtop FT-NMR System

BERLIN, Germany – August 25, 2019 – At EUROISMAR 2019, Bruker announced the European launch of the Fourier 80 system, a next-generation, high-performance 80 MHz Fourier Transform nuclear magnetic resonance (FT-NMR) benchtop spectrometer. The Fourier 80 has been designed for organic or medicinal chemistry research, routine analysis, teaching or synthesis verification in any chemistry laboratory.
